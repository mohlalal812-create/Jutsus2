Quick reference for common actions in JUSTUS.

ActionHow to do it
View profileLong-press (hold) on any chat in the list
Mute notificationsIn profile, tap the mute toggle
Clear chatIn profile, tap "Clear Chat"
Update statusGo to Updates tab → tap "Add Status Update"
Make a callGo to Calls tab → tap the phone icon
Change themeSettings → Theme
Back to chatsTap the ← arrow in top left
💡 TipLong-press means pressing and holding a chat for about one second until the profile opens.
